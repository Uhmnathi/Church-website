OUR DECLARATIONS

I Am A Believer, I Am A Son, I Am A Chosen Generation - Royal Priesthood - A Holy Nation - God's Own Possession - That I May Proclaim The Excellencies Of The One Who Has Called Me Out Of Darkness Into His Marvelous Light. I Am A King, I Possess Overflowing Grace And The Gift Of Righteousness. I Don t Leave Life To Chance - I Live And Rule In Life As A King Under Christ's Kingship.

I Am Never Counted Amongst The Defeated-Others May Fail But Not Me. I Live Upward, Forward From Strength To Strength. I Am A Testimony Of God's Faithfulness. I Am An Altar Of Heaven On Earth. I Don't Speak, Think And Act Like The World, When Souls Are Cast Down All Around Me, I Decree A Lifting.

Today We Are Gathered Together As Living Stones Built Up As A Spiritual House For A Holy Priesthood Where Jesus Christ Reigns As King. Today We Are Gathered To Search Out Matters Related To Our Kingdom. This Is A Domain Of Heavenly Possibilities,

OUR MISSION AND VISION

VISION

We are a spiritual home where Jesus Christ reigns as King. (Ps29:10-11)

MISSION

1. We are called to raise a generation of kings who are fully submitted to the Lordship and Kingship of Jesus Christ. Is40:12, Dan7:14, Job42:2,1546:8-11, 1Cor8:5-6, Phil2:10-11, Is45:21-23, Jn12:32
2. We are called to raise a generation that makes pursuing God's Kingdom and His righteousness a priority. Mt6:33, Rom8:32. Mt13:44, Lk12:32, Micah 6:8, Is64:6, I545:8, Rom3:10-12
3. We are called to transform the kingdoms of this world into the Kingdom of our God. Dan7:18, 27-28, Lk19:13, Rev5:10, Gen1:26

We are a spiritual home where Jesus Christ reigns as King.
