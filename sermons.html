SHUN EVIL COUNSEL AND HEED THE CALL OF WISDOM!

The book of Proverbs teaches us to hear the instructions of the father, and not to forsake the law of the mother. When we do so we are able to resist the invitation of sinners ,and/or peer-pressure influences on us. We will not concede to going with the crowds when they participate in ungodly acts . Sinners tend to do evil in order to gain possessions and wealth , they do not mind spilling blood to achieve their goals. They are greedy and selfish and they are not aware that by doing so they are digging their own graves,and shorten their own lives, as their own greed kills them. We are warned not to use the same way they use to walk, and we should completely refrain from putting our feet on their paths. The best to do is to listen to the voice of Wisdom ! She always calls loudly from public places , i.e.city gates, open spaces and market places , where transactions take place . She guides us but we do not listen ! When you hear things chose what you want to take in ,also select what is good from things you see, and speak no evil with your mouth . If by seeing and hearing you chose evil counsel , it will come out of your mouth , and you will be seen as foolish . Never despise the knowledge and the call of Wisdom .
